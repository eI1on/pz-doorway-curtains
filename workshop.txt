title=[B41/B42] Doorway Curtains
description=[h1] Doorway Curtains [/h1]
description=
description=This mod allows you to place walk-through curtains on doorways, making it easy to pass through without needing to open them. Adds a practical, immersive detail to your base.
description=
description=To craft curtains, you'll need the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3067798182]Building Menu[/url] mod, which enables curtain crafting. Otherwise, curtains must be collected from the game world.
description=
description=[h1] Important Note: [/h1]
description=If using the Building Menu, load it [b]before[/b] this mod to be sure curtains are placed correctly on doorframes.
description=
description=[b] Key Features: [/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] Walk-Through Curtains: Curtains you can walk through without opening.
description=[*] Dynamic Placement Validity: Curtains snap to doorframes and window frames and can only be placed in valid positions, avoiding interference with other objects. Also curtains are snapping to the doorframes, just like to the window frames.
description=[/list]
